# Privacy Policy for Ramify

Ramify is a client-side web application. All data you create is stored locally
in your browser using LocalStorage/IndexedDB. No data is transmitted to or
stored on any external server controlled by the developer.

- We do not collect, track, or share any personal information.  
- Your data stays entirely on your device.  
- If you clear your browser storage, your data will be deleted.  

By using Ramify, you agree to this privacy policy.
